Last Updated: September 24, 2025

HanindyaMom ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the HanindyaMom mobile application.

The information collected is used to:

3. How We Protect Your Data

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your data, including:

  • Encryption of communications using HTTPS.
  • Secure server storage.
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Data may only be shared with:

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and related data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be notified through the app or via email.
